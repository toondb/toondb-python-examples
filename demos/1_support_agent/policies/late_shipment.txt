# Late Shipment Policy

## Policy Overview
When a customer reports a shipment delay, agents should follow these guidelines to resolve the issue promptly and maintain customer satisfaction.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Order is more than 2 business days past the estimated delivery date
- Customer has not previously received a replacement/refund for this order
- Order value is under $500 (escalate to manager for higher values)

## Resolution Options

### Option 1: Reroute to Alternative Address
- Available if package is still in transit
- Customer must provide valid alternative delivery address
- No additional charge to customer
- Update delivery address in system immediately
- Send confirmation email with new ETA

### Option 2: Replacement Order
- Available for orders still more than 3 days from delivery
- Ship replacement via expedited shipping (1-2 day)
- Original order will be canceled upon delivery or returned
- Customer preference: check user profile for "replacements_over_refunds" flag
- Requires manager approval for orders over $200

### Option 3: Refund
- Last resort option if reroute/replacement not viable
- Full refund to original payment method
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- Customer keeps original order if it arrives

## Special Circumstances
- **Traveling customers**: Prioritize reroute or expedited replacement
- **Time-sensitive items**: Offer refund + expedited replacement credit
- **Repeat issues**: Flag account for investigation

## Required Actions
1. Create support ticket with issue details
2. Update order status in system
3. Log resolution in customer audit trail
4. Send confirmation email to customer
